On the user research side, I designed surveys and conducted contextual interviews and ethnographic research. Our team's focus was on using data to test our design assumptions.

The Design Problem

College students have the lowest voter turnout of any age demographic. How can we utilize the human centered design process to leverage this population and create more active citizens? Our team designed snapchat filters that informed students where their polling place was. This solution is a way to disperse information to a wide array of college students through a medium that is convenient in their daily lives.
